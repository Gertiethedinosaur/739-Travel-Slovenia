Things To Do

Postojna Jama is stands out from other caves due to the fact that there is a resort. See the baby dragons and castles, ride on a minecart, and stay at the hotel with a giftshop and cafeteria. There are other huge caves around the country so if you can see more than one! The mountains and snow are also great for skiing and snowboarding.


If you're looking for city life Ljublijana is the place you want to be. The capital is where you're going to find the most going on, and discover shops and museums. Koper is home to a blend of Slovic and Italian culture, not too far from Trieste in Italy. Koper has shopping and the seaside, so there's something for everyone.
